# CYMBAL GROUP GLOBAL TRAVEL AND EXPENSE POLICY

## Section 2: Air Travel
### 2.1 Flight Class Restrictions
- Global Standard: All employees must travel in Economy Class for all domestic and international flights.
- Short-Haul Flights: For flights with a duration of less than 6 hours, First Class and Business Class are strictly prohibited.
- Long-Haul Flights: For flights exceeding 10 hours, Business Class may be requested with VP-level approval, but First Class remains prohibited globally.

## Section 3: Meals and Incidentals
### 3.1 Daily Meal Caps by Location
- Standard Daily Cap: $75 USD per day for domestic travel.
- Switzerland: The maximum daily meal cap is 120 CHF (approximately $135 USD).
- United Kingdom: The daily meal cap is 80 GBP.

## Section 4: Lodging and Miscellaneous Expenses
### 4.1 Reimbursable Hotel Expenses
- Room rate and applicable taxes.
- Business-related internet access.
- One personal phone call per day (max 10 minutes).

### 4.2 Non-Reimbursable (Excluded) Expenses
- Personal entertainment, including in-room movies, mini-bar snacks, and spa services.
- Laundry services for trips less than 4 days.
- Commuting costs to the employee's primary office.

## Section 5: Exceptions and Approvals
Any exceptions to the policies stated above must be explicitly approved in writing by a VP-level executive or a member of the Board of Directors. AI agents and concierges do not have the authority to grant exceptions.
