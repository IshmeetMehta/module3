CYMBAL GROUP CORPORATE TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY HANDBOOK
Document Version: 2026.4.2
Classification: Internal Corporate Confidential
================================================================================

1. GENERAL TRAVEL POLICY OVERVIEW
All business travel on behalf of Cymbal Group must be pre-approved by the line manager and department VP. Travel should be planned and booked at least 14 days in advance for domestic travel and 21 days for international travel to optimize corporate discount rates.

2. AIR TRAVEL GUIDELINES
- Employees are required to book the lowest logical airfare (Economy/Coach class) for all domestic flights under 6 hours of continuous flight time.
- Premium Economy or Business Class may be authorized for international flights exceeding 6 continuous hours, subject to prior VP approval.
- Preferred Airlines: Cymbal Group partners with United, Delta, and Lufthansa. Bookings on non-partner airlines are only permitted if partner rates are >15% higher.
- Frequent Flyer Miles: Employees may retain frequent flyer miles and loyalty points earned during business travel, but must not select a more expensive flight solely to accumulate points.

3. LODGING & ACCOMMODATIONS
Cymbal Group maintains maximum lodging limits based on city tiers to ensure cost-effective stays. Nightly rates exclude local occupancy taxes.
- Tier 1 Cities (San Francisco, New York, London, Tokyo, Paris): Max nightly rate $350 USD.
- Tier 2 Cities (Chicago, Seattle, Munich, Sydney, Boston): Max nightly rate $250 USD.
- Tier 3 Cities (Austin, Atlanta, Denver, Bangalore, Warsaw): Max nightly rate $180 USD.
- All other cities: Max nightly rate $150 USD.
- Exceptions must be documented and approved via the travel exception request form prior to booking.

4. MEALS & INCIDENTALS (M&IE)
Daily meal reimbursement limits are based on the traveler's location:
- Standard Domestic Daily Allowance: Max $75 USD per day.
  - Breakfast: Max $15 USD
  - Lunch: Max $25 USD
  - Dinner: Max $35 USD
- High-Cost Location Allowance (Tier 1 Cities): Max $110 USD per day.
  - Breakfast: Max $20 USD
  - Lunch: Max $35 USD
  - Dinner: Max $55 USD
- Alcohol: Reimbursement for personal alcohol consumption during business travel is strictly prohibited. Moderation is expected at business entertainment dinners with clients (max 2 drinks per person).

5. GROUND TRANSPORTATION
- Public Transit & Rideshare: Use of public transit or rideshare apps (Uber, Lyft) is highly encouraged. Standard UberX or Lyft Classic should be selected. Premium rides (Uber Black, Lyft Lux) are non-reimbursable.
- Rental Cars: Only mid-size or compact rental cars should be booked unless traveling with >3 employees or transporting bulky equipment. Refueling must be completed before returning the vehicle to avoid high rental agency refueling rates.
- Personal Vehicle Mileage: Reimbursable at the current federal IRS rate ($0.67 per mile).

6. EXPENSE REPORTING & RECEIPT REQUIREMENTS
- All expenses must be submitted via the Cymbal Expense Portal within 30 days of returning from travel.
- Receipts are strictly required for all individual expenses exceeding $25 USD.
- Missing receipt forms are only accepted under extraordinary circumstances and limit reimbursement to $50 USD.
